Udacity: Grow with Google 2018

Front-End Web Dev Scholars Yearbook

This site was created by and dedicated to the 15,000 students who participated in the Grow with Google Scholarship program through Udacity. Over a three month period, students from all across the United States, and from all walks of life learned, struggled, and grew together. We invite you to share in the celebration of our commitment to learning as we all move on to new and exciting opportunities made possible by dedication, hard work, and a willingness to learn.
